What our previous visitors says...

Home of Indigenous Arts, Paintings, Artifacts and excellent Architecture. Well maintained and perfect for relaxation, photography and Art Appreciation. Visit the place early so you'll have enough time to tour the place. It's a safe haven to the eyes of an Artist. Thumbs up!

- Ivy Aguas

Pinto Art Museum is one of the hidden gems of Rizal province. Once you step into the museum, you will be transported to another place that is not in the Philippines. The rustic and Mediterranean vibe elevates the experience of art appreciation; the simple yet majestic addition of the bell tower in the 'Pinto Academy of Wholeness' (can't really remember the name) was astounding.

- Ricardo Dalisay

So nice to be here. A great place for relaxing mind and heart.. Paintings are really amazing 'cuz it shows and reflects the reality of our world from the past up to now. Thanks Pinto Art Museum, the entrance fee is enough to feel and see the great ambiance and cool places.

- Juan Dela Cruz
